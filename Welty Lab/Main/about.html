About the Welty Lab

Over the last three decades, digital technologies have brought foundational changes—and, with those changes, new challenges—to literary scholarship and historical preservation. In this new environment, the custodians of Eudora Welty’s legacy have a new challenge. We must imagine and build a robust, multimedia platform that will present her work to audiences that, for worse and better, increasingly prefer electronic to printed media. If we do not plan now for Welty’s digital future, then we risk sowing the seeds of her obsolescence. Literary history reminds us time and again that, in the long view, an author’s survival depends upon scholars who transmit her work from generation to generation and interpret it anew for their own time.

Understanding not just one but many places, Welty has helped us, the “sweet devourers” of her fictions, understand all places better. She knew, as she herself put it, that “a place that ever was lived in is like a fire that never goes out … the result of some original ignition.” How can we help ensure that the flame of her imagination endures to ignite the understanding to the readers of 2028, 2058, 2118?
